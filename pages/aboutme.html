About me!

From the moment I first laid hands on a computer, I knew that programming was my true calling, my destined path to greatness. The endless possibilities of this machine captivated me, and I found myself exploring every inch of its power. Programming has always been my passion, the key to unlocking the full potential of this remarkable invention and bending it to my will.

Growing up was tough, especially after the loss of our father at such a young age. I had to step up and take on the responsibility of providing for my family and supporting our household. For years, I dedicated myself to mastering the skills required to succeed in business, studying everything from foreign trade and sales to marketing, hydraulics, and mechanics. I became a specialized technician, traveling the world to take technical courses in different factories.

But deep down, I always knew that there was something more waiting for me, a higher purpose that I was destined to fulfill. And it wasn't until I became a husband and father myself, at my 35 years of existence, that I realized what that purpose truly was. I want to be a programmer, a creator of worlds, a master of technology. The future is mine for the taking, and I am ready to seize it with all the passion and determination that has driven me from the very beginning.
